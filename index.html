Carter Zhang

I am Carter Zhang - a senior high school student living in Harbin, China. Certainly, you can call me Lovely. I am fond of Information Technology, and I am a supporter of free software. I love free software, therefore I have Firefox, GIMP, Kdenlive, VLC, etc. installed on my computer. Even my avatar, which is shown on the left, as well as the cover picture of my webpage, are both from a free and open source webcomic named Pepper & Carrot. What's more, I use Debian instead of Windows, just for it's free software, as in freedom.




An Free Software Lover 

I am a free software lover, believing in the GNU philosophy. More details.

Being different from most people that use Google Chrome, I use Mozilla Firefox as my web browser. With Gecko inside, Firefox gives me a fast & relievable experience of surfing the Internet. Moreover, I use GIMP instead of Photoshop to edit photos, Kdenlive instead of Premiere to create & edit videos, LibreOffice and ONLYOFFICE instead of Microsoft Office to create & edit documents, etc.

In my view, proprietary software is evil. It doesn't respect users' freedom and community, and put its developers' power over the users.
