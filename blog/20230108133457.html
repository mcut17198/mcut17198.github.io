Lovely's Live v1.0 is published!

Lovely's Live is an Live environment used to rescue your operating system, as a free software (as in freedom) alternative to Hiren's BootCD PE.

To use Lovely's Live, extract the archive, put the folder lovely-live in your USB drive, and execute /lovely-live/boot/bootinst.sh (\lovely-live\boot\bootinst.bat for Windows) to make it bootable.
