(Important) The Announcement that I will be Deregistering Most of My Social/IM Services

Dear Friends,

Hello, it’s Carter.

To respond to the Free Software Movement launched by the Free Software Foundation and struggle to reach the goal of getting my computers and other devices running 100% free software, I will be deregistering most of my proprietary social/IM services. The schedule is shown below.

  • SnapChat: February 12, 2023
  • WhatsApp: February 12, 2023
  • Facebook: July 20, 2023
  • Discord: July 25, 2023
  • Instagram: July 27, 2023
  • Twitter: July 28, 2023
  • Skype: July 30, 2023 (Just delete all contacts and sign out, the Microsoft Account won’t be closed)
  • Microsoft Teams: July 30, 2023 (Just delete all contacts and sign out, the Microsoft Account won’t be closed)
  • WeChat (lovely17198): July 7, 2024 (begin preparation) July 19, 2024 (apply for deregistration)
  • WeChat (lovely17199): July 20, 2024 (begin preparation) July 22, 2024 (apply for deregistration)
  • ICQ New: July 27, 2024
  • Telegram: January 26, 2025

After the deregistration of these services, I will be using Matrix.org, which is secure, open source and free as in speech, mainly as my IM solution.

My account is: @mcut17198:comm.cx

If you want to register Matrix.org service, just install a Matrix.org client and follow the instructions to register. The process is simple. Element or FluffyChat are recommended for your mobile devices and NeoChat is recommended for PCs or laptops. I recommended the server comm.cx for those who lives in China and matrix.org for those who lives outside China.

Sorry for the inconvenience. However, software freedom is essential for computer users including me and is worth fighting for. Therefore, I have made the decision to deregister these proprietary social/IM services, in order to get rid of proprietary software.

In future, I will devote myself in the Free Software Movement.

Best wishes!

Yours,

Carter Zhang
